Our Heritage

Three Decades of Tradition

Navinya Silks began in 2024 with a single loom and a vision to preserve the dying art of handwoven silk. Founded by a family of weavers, we have spent 30 years bridging the gap between ancient craftsmanship and modern elegance.

We work directly with over 200 master artisans, ensuring that every saree is a masterpiece of authentic Zari and pure silk.

The Craft

The Weaving Rhythm

01. Warping

The vertical 'Warp' threads are aligned on the loom, creating the structural foundation of the silk.

02. Shedding

Artisans lift specific threads to create a 'shed'—the tunnel where the design begins to breathe.

03. Picking

The horizontal 'Weft' thread (shuttle) is passed through, interlocking the silk and Zari patterns.
